What I actually do

I turn stories into visibility, and visibility into connection.

My work often starts with a simple question: who needs to see this story, and why should they care? From there, I shape the message, create the content, plan the distribution and look at the results.

I’m comfortable moving between strategy and execution: writing captions, editing videos, organizing interviews, planning events, building partnerships, analyzing performance and making sure the final result feels native to the platform and true to the brand.
