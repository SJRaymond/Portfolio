Paulinessence · Sewing Patterns Brand

Paulinessence is the sewing pattern brand I founded to make fashion more accessible through digital products, education, and community-driven content.

What started as a personal passion for sewing evolved into a growing online business combining product development, content creation, and digital strategy.

Through Paulinessence, I built a brand that connects creativity, technical skills, and entrepreneurship.

The Concept

The goal of Paulinessence is to help people create their own clothing through simple, well-designed sewing patterns and accessible instructions.

  • Digital sewing patterns (English & French)
  • Instruction guides and tutorials
  • Content designed to educate and inspire
  • Community-driven brand growth

Product & Content

Digital sewing patterns designed for accessibility and creativity

  • Product development — created and launched 8–10 sewing pattern products designed for beginner to intermediate users.

Content creation and community engagement

  • Content strategy — developed social media content to educate, inspire, and promote products through tutorials, tips, and behind-the-scenes content.

E-commerce website and user experience

  • Website management — built and managed the website, focusing on SEO, user experience, and conversion optimization.

Growth & Results

Brand growth through content and digital strategy

  • 8–10 product launches
  • Strong engagement driven by educational and community-focused content
  • Successful influencer and UGC collaborations
