Founder Project

Paulinessence Sewing Patterns Brand

Paulinessence is the sewing pattern brand I founded to make fashion more accessible through digital products, education, and community-driven content.

Back to portfolio Get in touch

What started as a personal passion for sewing evolved into a growing online business combining product development, content creation, and digital strategy.

The goal of Paulinessence is to help people create their own clothing through simple, well-designed sewing patterns and accessible instructions.

Through the brand, I connected creativity, technical skills, e-commerce, and entrepreneurship.
