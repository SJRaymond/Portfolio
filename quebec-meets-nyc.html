Québec Meets NYC

I’m someone who naturally brings people together, which led me to build a Québec community here in NYC.

What started as a storytelling project quickly evolved into a growing network connecting Québécois, francophones, and allies through interviews, cultural content, and in-person events.

Today, the project serves as a platform to create bridges between Québec and NYC by showcasing real people, real stories, and real opportunities.

From storytelling to community

I initially started Québec Meets NYC by sharing stories of Québécois living in New York.

Very quickly, the content resonated beyond expectations and turned into something bigger: a space where people connect, collaborate, and see themselves reflected in others’ journeys.

  • New collaborations
  • A growing audience across platforms
  • Real-life events bringing the community together in NYC
