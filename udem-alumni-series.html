Alumni Storytelling

Universite de Montreal Alumni Interview Series

As part of Quebec Meets NYC, I collaborated with Universite de Montreal to highlight the impact of alumni living and working in New York City.

Back to portfolio Quebec Meets NYC

Bilingual Series

NYC

Stories connecting Quebec education, professional growth, and international alumni visibility.

The series showcased Quebec talent abroad through personal stories, career journeys, and the international reach of UdeM graduates.

I developed a video interview format featuring UdeM alumni based in NYC, focusing on their path from Quebec to New York and the role their education played in their journey.

The content connected education, professional growth, and Quebec's presence in New York across social platforms and long-form storytelling.

Content Strategy

One series, multiple audiences.

The series was designed to live across multiple platforms, with each format adapted to the audience and platform behavior.

  • Full interviews published on YouTube.
  • Short previews adapted for LinkedIn and social media.
  • Captions written to highlight alumni impact and Quebec visibility.
  • Calls to action created to support alumni engagement.
